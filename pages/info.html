Information

This is a narrative-based, interactive website that is centered on the recent wave of Asian hate. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Asians have received unjustified hate, which have led to several cases of Asian hate crimes.

You will play as an Asian American living in New York City. You will be presented with different options throughout the story, which have different outcomes.

This project was created by Kevin Zhong, Bryan Kuncahyo, and Nicolas Wong. Through this experience, we hope people will gain a new perspective.

Background & Context

This is a second-person narrative-based, interactive website that is centered on the recent wave of Asian hate. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, Asians in the United States have received an increased amount of unjustified hate, which has led to several cases of Asian hate crimes. Our project aims to address this particular kind of hate in a way that is (hopefully) more interactive and engaging than traditional methods.

Furthermore, the main purpose of the project is for other people to understand Asian hate crimes from a victim's perspective.

This project was created by Kevin Zhong, Bryan Kuncahyo, and Nicolas Wong for the Humanities 201 Creative Project.

Directions

In story, you will play as an Asian American who goes about their day. You will be presented with different options throughout the story, which have different outcomes. The endings you reach are dependent on the choices you make along the story.
